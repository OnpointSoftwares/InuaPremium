Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our loan offers? We've got answers. If you have any other queries, feel free to contact us.

1. What are the eligibility criteria for applying for a loan?

Eligibility criteria may vary depending on the loan type. Generally, you must be at least 18 years old, have a steady source of income, and meet other specific requirements of the loan type.

2. How long does the loan approval process take?

The loan approval process usually takes between 24 to 48 hours after submission of all required documents.

3. What documents are required to apply for a loan?

You will typically need to provide identification (such as a passport or ID card), proof of income (such as payslips or bank statements), and proof of address (such as a utility bill).

4. Can I repay my loan early?

Yes, you can repay your loan early without any penalties. Early repayment can help you save on interest costs.

5. How do I track my loan repayment?

You can track your loan repayment through our online portal or by contacting our customer service team for assistance.
